Task: What is the next planned action for !<INPUT 10>! to balance their stats and fulfill their daily role/goals? 
Choose one action category from: "Consume" (eat/drink food from inventory), "Gather" (harvest items or prepare food from a nearby resource tile), "Rest" (recover stamina at a bed/sofa), "Work" (perform professional or daily duties), "Socialize" (chat or interact with other personas), "Recreate" (relax, watch TV, play games, read), or "Idle" (do nothing).
Also specify the target object/person name (e.g. "refrigerator", "bed", "stove", the name of another resident, or a food item in inventory), and the duration of the action in minutes (between 10 and 120 minutes). Write a descriptive "detail" representing the action's actual description (e.g., "having a light lunch", "brewing coffee", "chatting with Klaus Mueller", "writing a research paper").

Response format:
Respond ONLY in valid JSON format:
{
  "action": "<Consume/Gather/Rest/Work/Socialize/Recreate/Idle>",
  "target": "<target_object_or_person_name>",
  "detail": "<descriptive action description string>",
  "duration": <integer_duration_in_minutes>,
  "reasoning": "<brief explanation of the choice based on current stats and roles>"
}
Do not include any explanation outside of the JSON block.
